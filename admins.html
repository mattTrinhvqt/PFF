Onboarding for new admins

1. PFF admin Google group

You have been added to the PFF admin Google group, which receives new ReportIt submissions and emails sent to admin@phoropterfreefridays.org.

Use ReportIt evidence for formal complaints to regulatory bodies. Check with the submitter before identifying anyone.

View the complaints master list .

2. Set up Facebook

Join AEIOU for current HSU optometry activity.

Help review PFF join requests. Check AHPRA/Google to confirm optometrists are employees. Independent owners are okay unless an employee objects. Students should email us from their student address to confirm it is active.

Use the PhoroptersSattoThurs Facebook account for admin posts. Remind Matt if you have not been granted access yet.

3. Join the Facebook Messenger chats

There is a social chat and an admin tasks chat. The admin tasks chat is the important one. Everything is voluntary, so contribute when you can.

4. Become an HSU delegate (optional)

Contact your state HSU rep if you want to formally support employees as a union delegate. Contacts are at the bottom of the PFF website .

Main goal: encourage optometrists to speak up. We are here to facilitate.
