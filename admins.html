PFF Admins

Onboarding for new admins

1. PFF admin Google group

The group receives new ReportIt submissions and emails sent to admin@phoropterfreefridays.org. Use ReportIt evidence or testimonies for formal complaints or your own reports. Check with the submitter before identifying them.

2. Set up Facebook

  • Join AEIOU for current HSU optometry activity.
  • Help review PFF join requests. Check AHPRA/Google to confirm optometrists are employees. Independent owners are okay unless an employee objects. Students should use their student email.
  • Use the PhoroptersSattoThurs Facebook account for admin posts. Remind Matt if you have not been granted access yet.

3. Join the Facebook Messenger chats

There is a social chat and an admin tasks chat. The admin tasks chat is the important one. Everything is voluntary, so contribute when you can.

4. Become an HSU delegate (optional)

Contact your state HSU rep if you want to formally support employees as a union delegate. Contacts are on the PFF website .

Main goal: encourage optometrists to speak up. We are here to help facilitate it.
